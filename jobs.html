PhD Studentship Positions

The call for the PhD studentship position at the Universtiy of Torino are now open. You can find the link here .
  • One position is reserved for students with a non-Italian degree. The selection will be made based only on CVs.
  • Eight positions are open to anybody. The selection will be made based on both the CV and the result of an admission test.
The deadline for the application is July, 2 at 12 o'clock. Further information on the Torino Graduate School in Physics and Astrophysics are available here . If you have any question, do not hesitate to contact one of the persons listed in people (preferably, Prof. Marco Costa and/or Prof. Ernesto Migliore).
